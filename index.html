About Our Games

Real Flight Simulator

Real Flight Simulator offers an authentic flying experience with detailed aircraft models and realistic physics. Players can explore various environments, from bustling cities to serene countryside, while mastering different aircraft types.

How to play: Use W, A, S, D keys for basic movement, mouse for direction, and Space to accelerate. Complete challenges or enjoy free flight mode to experience the thrill of aviation.

Drift King

Drift King puts you behind the wheel of high-performance cars designed for the perfect drift. With multiple tracks and vehicles to unlock, you'll master the art of controlled sliding while competing against the clock.

How to play: Arrow keys or W, A, S, D to control your car. Hold Space while turning to initiate drifts. Earn points for longer, more controlled drifts and unlock new cars and tracks as you progress.

Masked Special Forces

Masked Special Forces is a tactical shooter that puts you in the boots of an elite soldier. Navigate through enemy territory, complete objectives, and eliminate threats with precision in this action-packed game.

How to play: Use W, A, S, D to move, mouse to aim and shoot. R to reload, E to interact with objects, and C to crouch. Plan your approach carefully and use cover to survive intense firefights.

Basket Hoop

Basket Hoop brings the excitement of basketball to your browser with simple yet addictive gameplay. Perfect your timing and accuracy to sink shots from increasing distances and angles.

How to play: Click and hold to set power, release to shoot. Adjust your angle by moving the mouse. Score consecutive baskets to increase your multiplier and achieve the highest score possible.
